ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Anteneh Yitayal. I completed my undergraduate studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a focus on Computer Engineering at Addis Ababa Science and Technology Univesity (AASTU) in September, 2021. I have a strong interest in the application of machine learning and deep learning models in the fields of finance and algorithm fairness.

During my time at AASTU, I had the opportunity to work on a research project under the guidance of Dereje Yohanness, an Associate Professor. The project focused on the detection of counterfeit banknotes using deep learning techniques. This research was funded by AASTU, and it provided me with valuable hands-on experience in developing and implementing machine learning algorithms.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I enjoy challenging myself by solving coding challenges on platforms like HackerRank, LeetCode, and Codeforces. These activities have helped me enhance my problem-solving skills and strengthen my understanding of algorithms and data structures.

Outside of my academic and coding endeavors, I have a passion for football and movies. I find great joy in watching football matches and following the latest news and updates in the world of sports. Additionally, I appreciate the power of storytelling in movies and enjoy exploring various genres. I believe that conducting thorough machine learning research is time-consuming but ultimately rewarding. I am excited to continue my journey in this field, exploring new applications and contributing to the advancement of technology.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I look forward to new opportunities and collaborations in the exciting world of machine learning.
